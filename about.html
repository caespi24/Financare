Our Mission

The mission at hand is to guarantee universal and fair accessibility to both healthcare and financial information, fostering an environment where individuals are empowered to make well-informed decisions regarding their overall well-being. This imperative underscores a commitment to dismantling barriers that may impede access to essential healthcare services and pertinent financial knowledge. By doing so, the mission seeks to create a level playing field where every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, can exercise their right to make informed choices about their health and financial security. The emphasis on equity in access implies a dedication to addressing disparities and ensuring that information and resources are distributed impartially, ultimately aiming to uplift communities and promote a society where everyone has the tools and knowledge necessary to actively participate in decisions that profoundly impact their lives.

Our Story

Concerns about the well-being of Zamboanga's population arise due to parallels between Macario Tiu's "Balyan" story and local realities. Like in the narrative, people in Zamboanga seek traditional healing from Datu Pikong, posing health risks. To address these issues, we were inspired to implement "FinanCare," a digital platform ensuring equal access to healthcare and financial education. The initiative aims to make healthcare services accessible to all, transcending marginalized areas. Ultimately, the goal is to establish an equitable healthcare system for widespread treatment accessibility.

Our Vision

The envisioned future for Zamboanga City is one characterized by inclusivity and equality, where every individual, irrespective of their socio-economic status, is granted equal access to high-quality healthcare and comprehensive financial knowledge. This aspirational vision underscores a commitment to breaking down the barriers that often restrict certain segments of the population from enjoying essential healthcare services and gaining crucial financial insights. In this future scenario, the city aims to cultivate a community that is not only physically healthy but also financially empowered, recognizing the intrinsic connection between health and financial well-being. By ensuring that each person, regardless of their background, has the same opportunities to access healthcare resources and financial education, the vision seeks to create a harmonious and equitable society where the overall health and financial literacy of the community are elevated. This harmonized approach envisions a Zamboanga City where the collective well-being of its residents is prioritized and where every individual can thrive, contributing to the city's prosperity and shared success.
